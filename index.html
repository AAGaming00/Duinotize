Why should you use this?

Your money belongs to you

With Mineuino your money is taken straight from your users to your wallet, we do nothing with it in the middle, we're just the javascript getting it to you. There is no third party, no middleman, just you and your money.

Environment friendly

duino-coin uses environment friendly SHA-1 hashes, making the entire chain use less electricity than a household.

Every device generates revenue

due to the rise of mobile devices, monetizers get more than half their users from mobile devices. Duino-coin is prepared, with the next gen kolka system, designed to make all devices good miners and revenue creators.

Anti-Virus safe

So far, AVG, avast and other anti viruses do not consider this a trojan, and it is not, check out the source code on github at vatsadev/mineuino.

No user tracking

With the rise of web3 and privacy, users should remain anonymous, and mineuino is prepared, making sure there are no trackers, and that no user details are revealed in the miner

Uses far less battery than ads

As brave browser has shown, ads take up 35% more battery than needed, and mineuino understands battery concerns, but duino-coin mining wont even turn on your laptop fan, as the gpu usage is very low.

All protocols

we understand that your website may use http, https, or ipfs and we support multiple protocols.

Crypto Currencies

You get paid out in duino-coin, a environment friendly currency

Helps retentive sites, not clickbaits

As mining requires time, the more time a user is engaged at your site, the more you earn, while 2 second clickbaits earn almost nothing
